A Little Bit About Me

Hello! My name is Sam and I am a second year programming student. I made the switch to Computer Science from Graphic Design because I enjoy learning how to code a lot more than learn about art. While I still love making my own art and designing in my free time, I've always had an interest in STEM related courses. Lately I've really loved learning how to program; I could even say I've grown a passion for it!

As far as my hobbies go, I work on a lot of digital art and graphic design projects in my free time. I draw a lot of video game characters.
